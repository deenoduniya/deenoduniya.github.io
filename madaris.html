Madaris

Traditional Islamic institutions for Quran and Islamic studies.

Nurturing Islamic Knowledge

Our Madaris provide a structured environment for students to learn the Quran with proper tajweed, memorize the Holy Book (Hifz), understand its translation (Tarjuma), and study foundational Islamic sciences.

Qualified teachers guide students through Nazra and Hifz programs, while advanced courses like Muallim-e-Deen prepare those who wish to become Islamic educators and scholars.

Programs Offered

  • Hifz (Quran Memorization)
  • Nazra (Recitation)
  • Tarjuma (Translation)
  • Muallim-e-Deen
