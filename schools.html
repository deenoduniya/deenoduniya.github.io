Schools

Quality Education for All Ages

Deenoduniya schools offer a full academic curriculum from KG1 through Grade 10, combined with Islamic studies and character development. Students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for higher studies and life.

Our curriculum aligns with national standards while integrating Quran and Islamic values throughout the school day. Small class sizes and qualified teachers ensure personalized attention.
